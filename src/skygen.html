Website redesign

Just the mention of these words is enough to send a shiver down the spines of even the most seasoned business owners and marketers. Those who have been through it know that redesigning and developing a website is akin to an epic quest that is best avoided unless absolutely necessary.

But let me tell you about SKYGEN. This company is a shining example of how innovation and craftsmanship can lay the foundation for an unparalleled reputation in Canada and the United States. SKYGEN is not just about windows and doors; it's a whole collection of pergolas and sliding systems, each distinguished by exceptional quality. They pride themselves on long-term relationships with clients, high standards, and the use of the latest technologies at every stage of their work. And here comes the Brosla team, ready to help them conquer the digital space.

So, if you thought your website was perfect and didn't need any changes, think again. Perhaps our team can turn your digital nightmare into a triumph.

Imagine...

juggling flaming torches while riding a unicycle on a tightrope – that’s kind of like what Skygen does for its clients.

We're not just about windows and doors; we're about the intricate dance of regional quirks, payment preferences, local competitors, seasonal demands, and sizing charts. Yeah, we're basically the circus performers of the home improvement world.

Skygen’s solutions are like nothing you've ever seen – we're talking about a revolutionary reimagining of what’s possible in the windows and doors universe in Canada and the USA. We had to serve up the details of Skygen’s processes in bite-sized chunks that were both deliciously precise and effortlessly showcased their value without making anyone’s head explode.

With a mountain of research on Skygen's top competitors and countless heart-to-heart chats with the CEO and the team, the marketing wizards at Brosla whipped up a vibrant yet informative brand voice. This magic trick effectively communicated Skygen's value – and all without needing a crystal ball.

Powerful message, but the graphics?

Absolutely essential — maybe even the most crucial part — for wrapping up the Skygen story. We had to figure out the best way to visualize potential, so naturally, we built another site and linked them with a transitional page.

The end result?

A series of visual representations for each product or solution, combined with bespoke architecture. These visuals didn't just clearly outline Skygen's various offerings but also symbolized the highly customizable and unique websites Skygen builds for its clients.

With images, icons, and text doing the heavy lifting, the color palette and fonts had to play nice, supporting the information without stealing the spotlight. They also had to convey the simplicity of the client experience.

The design team presented Skygen with three color themes:

  • simple e-commerce

  • high fashion

  • urban

The animations on the site?

Oh, they're there too, showcasing smooth and simple moments of delight as you scroll through the content. The graphics guide the audience's gaze across the page, ensuring nothing gets lost in translation.
