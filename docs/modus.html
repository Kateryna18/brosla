Protection from External Damage.

Made from thin yet durable plastic, the Modus case shields your iMac from scratches and smudges. Inside, a soft, thin layer provides extra cushioning without trapping heat, keeping your device cool and protected.

The Modus case snugly wraps around the back of your Apple iMac, fitting its sleek lines perfectly. From the front, it’s almost invisible, preserving the elegant design of your iMac without cramping your style. But the real magic happens at the back. BROSLA takes immense pride in having such fantastic partners and wishes Modus nothing but success in their quest to become a unicorn company!
